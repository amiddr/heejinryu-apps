DishSnap

From photo to recipe

DishSnap lets you take or upload a photo of a dish or ingredients and instantly generates a recipe based on what you have.

You can optionally add notes such as dietary preferences or pantry level to tailor the recipe.

Pricing:
5 free recipe credits on install.
$1.99 for 30 additional recipe credits.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

  • Photos you submit to generate recipes
  • Optional text input you provide (such as preferences or notes)
  • An anonymous user identifier used to manage app functionality
  • Purchase information processed by Apple

How We Use Information

  • To generate recipes based on your photo and input
  • To manage usage limits and credit balances
  • To provide basic app functionality

Data Sharing

Photos and text you submit may be processed by third-party services, including AI providers, solely for the purpose of generating recipes.

In-app purchases are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive your payment details.

Data Storage

Photos are processed to generate recipes and are not stored permanently.

Recipe history is stored locally on your device and is not synced to a server.

Your Choices

You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

DishSnap is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
